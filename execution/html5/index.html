Yoga and Meditation

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India and aim to control and still the mind. Meditation is a practice in which an individual uses a technique of focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity and train attention and awareness. We always request our customers to involve in Yoga & Meditation Session, which we organise regularly.

Read More

Zumba

Zumba is a fitness program that involves cardio and Latin-inspired dance. Zumba is intended as a total-body cardio workout, which provides a large calorie consumption. Zumba is safe for all ages, meaning anyone can participate.

Read More

Laughter Yoga

Laughter yoga is a modern exercise involving prolonged voluntary laughter. It is usually done in groups, with eye contact and much playfulness between participants. Intentional laughter often turns into real and contagious laughter. We have a laughter club beside the gym area, where we organise laughter yoga every morning & evening.
